Why SpecCherry+

What is SpecCherry+?

I made this design for my own enjoyment, but many people have been interested in having one, my goal was to replace the existing Spectrum+ keyboard with something more reliable and modern and also to further my own skills.

Essentially it is a reimplementation of the ingenious master level of cost reducing 3 layer membrane as used on the original the spectrum+, but in PCB form. It uses Cherry MX switches (5-pin) and you can use Spectrum+ keys as they will press on to these quite firmly or you can add your own cherry keys. My own personal version connects to my Harlequin, I have replaced the membrane receptacles with Pin headers and used ribbon cables to connect the keyboard to the board. Feel free to experiment with your own solutions.
